# 27 March: Image Moderation, Social Lookups, Docs Overhaul

## NSFW Moderator API

We've launched an updated NSFW endpoint that uses AI moderation to screen images and GIFs uploaded through [flaunch.gg](http://flaunch.gg) and the Web 2 API.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (4).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

For tokens launched outside our infrastructure—whether at the contract level or through integrations—we monitor and review content retrospectively to allow, block, or blur NSFW images.
